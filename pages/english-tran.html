What is Transitive Verb?

A kind of Verb that need an Object to describe the Verb,

and complete the whole sentence.

Examples

1. Ms. Wong teachs.

1. Ms. Wong teachs English.


2. Benjimen eats.

2. Benjimen eats fish..


3. Man Hei plays.

3. Man Hei plays computer games..


Sentence Patterns

(Subject) + (Transitive Verb) + (Object)

What is Transitive Verb?

A kind of Verb that need an Object to Support it's meaning,

and complete the whole sentence.

Examples

1. Ms. Wong teachs.

1. Ms. Wong teachs English.


2. Benjimen eats.

2. Benjimen eats fish..


3. Man Hei plays.

3. Man Hei plays computer games..


Sentence Patterns

(Subject) + (Transitive Verb) + (Object)
